# Content Pillar Strategy Reference

Default frameworks, templates, and rotation schedules for the content engine. Use these when a brand does not have a defined content strategy.

---

## 1. Standard Pillar Frameworks

### The Core 4 Pillars (Universal Default)

Use this as the default when no brand strategy exists:

| Pillar | Purpose | Example Post Types |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Educate** | Teach the audience something useful | Tips, how-tos, tutorials, myth-busting, explainers |
| **Entertain** | Make the audience feel something positive | Memes, trends, behind-the-scenes, storytelling, humor |
| **Inspire** | Motivate action or aspiration | Case studies, testimonials, transformation stories, quotes |
| **Sell** | Drive conversions and revenue | Product demos, offers, CTAs, feature highlights, launches |

### The 5-Bucket Framework (Expanded)

For brands that need more variety:

| Pillar | Purpose | Weight |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Educational** | Build authority, teach skills | 25% |
| **Entertaining** | Humanize brand, drive shares | 20% |
| **Inspirational** | Motivate, build emotional connection | 15% |
| **Community** | Spark conversation, UGC, polls, Q&A | 20% |
| **Promotional** | Sell products/services directly | 20% |

### The 6-Bucket Framework (Full Spectrum)

For high-volume accounts posting daily:

| Pillar | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Educational** | Tips, tutorials, how-tos |
| **Entertaining** | Trends, memes, humor, pop culture |
| **Inspirational** | Success stories, transformations |
| **Personal/BTS** | Behind-the-scenes, day-in-the-life |
| **Conversational** | Polls, questions, hot takes, debates |
| **Promotional** | Products, offers, CTAs, launches |

## 2. Content Mix Ratios

### The 80/20 Rule (Conservative Default)

- **80% value-driven** content (educate + entertain + inspire + community)
- **20% promotional** content (sell)

Best for: Brands building awareness, early-stage companies, personal brands.

### The 60/25/15 Split (Balanced Default)

- **60% value-driven** (educational, problem-solving, informative)
- **25% entertaining** (relatable, trend-based, personality-driven)
- **15% promotional** (product, offers, CTAs)

Best for: Established brands with products to sell.

### The 70/20/10 Planning Framework

Controls how rigidly content is planned:

- **70% planned** content (scheduled pillar content, campaigns)
- **20% reactive** content (trending topics, news, cultural moments)
- **10% experimental** content (new formats, A/B tests, wild ideas)

### The Evergreen/Timely Split

- **60% evergreen** content (works anytime, compounding reach)
- **40% timely** content (seasonal, trending, event-driven)

Exception: E-commerce during holiday seasons flips to 80% seasonal / 20% evergreen.

## 4. Platform-Specific Pillar Weighting

Different pillars perform differently on each platform. Adjust weights accordingly:

### Instagram

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Lifestyle/Aspirational | Visual platform rewards aesthetic, aspirational content |
| Educational (Carousels) | Carousels outperform Reels on engagement rate and reach |
| Entertaining (Reels) | Reels get 67% more reach than static posts |
| UGC/Social Proof | Authentic user content drives saves and shares |

**Format priority**: Carousels > Reels > Static images > Stories
**Key metric**: Saves (strongest algorithm signal on Instagram)

### TikTok

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Entertaining | Platform culture is entertainment-first |
| Educational (Edutainment) | "Learn something" videos get high completion rates |
| Trending/Reactive | Algorithm rewards trend participation heavily |
| Personality/BTS | Raw, authentic content outperforms polished |

**Format priority**: Short video (15-60s) > longer video (1-3min)
**Key metric**: Watch time / completion rate (40-50% of algorithm weight)
**Note**: Content must be native. Videos reposted from other platforms get reduced distribution.

### LinkedIn

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Thought Leadership | Professional audience values original insights |
| Educational | Industry expertise builds authority |
| Social Proof/Case Studies | B2B buyers validate through peer results |
| Personal Stories | "What I learned" posts drive high engagement |

**Format priority**: Long-form text posts > document carousels > video > external links
**Key metric**: Comments within first hour (strongest algorithm signal)
**Note**: Algorithm penalizes external links. Keep content native.

### X (Twitter)

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Commentary/Hot Takes | Real-time discussion drives the platform |
| Educational Threads | Threads get high bookmark rates |
| Entertaining/Witty | Shareability is key on X |
| Community/Engagement | Replies and conversations boost visibility |

**Format priority**: Text > threads > images > video > links
**Key metric**: Replies and retweets

### Facebook

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Community Content | Groups and community interactions are prioritized |
| Video (Native) | Facebook pushes native video content |
| Entertaining | Shareable content reaches beyond followers |
| Local/Event Content | Strong for local business reach |

**Format priority**: Native video > images > text > links
**Key metric**: Shares (extends reach into new networks)

### YouTube

| Best Performing | Why |
|----------------|-----|
| Educational (Long-form) | Viewers expect depth and structure |
| Entertaining (Shorts) | Shorts drive subscriber growth |
| Tutorial/How-To | Evergreen search traffic |
| Social Proof | Testimonials and case studies |

**Format priority**: Long-form (8-15min) for depth, Shorts (< 60s) for reach
**Key metric**: Watch time and session duration

## 5. Pillar Rotation Strategies

### Weekly Rotation (Default for 3-5 posts/week)

Assign specific days to pillars to create a predictable rhythm:

**5-Day Rotation (Mon-Fri)**:
```
Monday    → Educational     (start the week with value)
Tuesday   → Social Proof    (build credibility)
Wednesday → Entertaining    (mid-week energy boost)
Thursday  → Community       (spark conversation)
Friday    → Promotional     (end-of-week CTA)
```

**3-Day Rotation (for lower frequency)**:
```
Day 1 → Educational
Day 2 → Entertaining / Personality
Day 3 → Promotional / Social Proof
```

**4-Day Rotation**:
```
Day 1 → Educational
Day 2 → Entertaining
Day 3 → Inspirational / Social Proof
Day 4 → Promotional
```

### Weekly Rotation with Weekend

**7-Day Rotation (daily posting)**:
```
Monday    → Educational (tip/tutorial)
Tuesday   → Social Proof (testimonial/case study)
Wednesday → Entertaining (trend/meme/BTS)
Thursday  → Educational (deep-dive/thread)
Friday    → Community (poll/question/engagement bait)
Saturday  → Personality (story/BTS/casual)
Sunday    → Promotional (soft sell/CTA)
```

### Monthly Rotation (4-Week Cycle)

Each week has a theme emphasis while still rotating pillars:

```
Week 1: AWARENESS    → Heavy on Educational + Entertaining
Week 2: CREDIBILITY  → Heavy on Social Proof + Case Studies
Week 3: ENGAGEMENT   → Heavy on Community + Personality
Week 4: CONVERSION   → Heavy on Promotional + Social Proof
```

### Anti-Fatigue Rules

- Never post the same pillar back-to-back
- Never post more than 2 promotional posts in a single week
- Ensure at least 1 entertaining/personality post per week
- Rotate formats within the same pillar (e.g., educational can be a carousel on Monday and a Reel on Thursday)

## 7. Engagement-Driven Pillar Selection

Different pillars drive different engagement actions. Choose based on goals:

### What Drives Comments

| Content Type | Why It Works |
|-------------|-------------|
| Hot takes / opinions | People want to agree or disagree |
| Questions / polls | Direct invitation to respond |
| "Unpopular opinion" posts | Controversy sparks discussion |
| Personal stories with a lesson | Relatability invites "me too" responses |
| "What would you do?" scenarios | Problem-solving is engaging |

**Best pillars**: Community, Personality, Thought Leadership

### What Drives Shares

| Content Type | Why It Works |
|-------------|-------------|
| Relatable memes / humor | "This is so me" sharing impulse |
| Valuable infographics | "My network needs to see this" |
| Inspiring quotes / stories | Emotional resonance |
| Data / statistics | Credibility-driven sharing |
| "Tag someone who..." | Direct sharing prompt |

**Best pillars**: Entertaining, Inspirational, Educational
**Impact**: Shared content reaches 3-5x more people than liked-only content

### What Drives Saves

| Content Type | Why It Works |
|-------------|-------------|
| Step-by-step tutorials | Reference material |
| Checklists / templates | Practical utility |
| Resource roundups | Bookmark for later |
| Frameworks / mental models | "I'll need this again" |
| Recipe-style how-tos | Actionable instructions |

**Best pillars**: Educational, Promotional (product tips)
**Impact**: Saves are the strongest signal on Instagram; indicate purchase intent

### What Drives Follows

| Content Type | Why It Works |
|-------------|-------------|
| Consistent niche expertise | "I want more of this" |
| Series content ("Part 1 of...") | Creates anticipation |
| Unique perspective/personality | Differentiation |
| High-production value | Perceived authority |

**Best pillars**: Educational, Personality, Thought Leadership

### Goal-to-Pillar Quick Reference

| Goal | Primary Pillars | Best Formats |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| Brand awareness | Entertaining, Educational | Reels, TikTok, Shorts |
| Community building | Community, Personality | Polls, Q&A, Lives, Stories |
| Lead generation | Educational, Social Proof | Carousels, threads, long-form |
| Direct sales | Promotional, Social Proof | Product demos, testimonials |
| Authority building | Educational, Thought Leadership | Long-form, case studies |

## 8. Seasonal and Trending Content Integration

### Two-Tier Content System

**Tier 1: Evergreen Pillar Content (60%)**
- Always-relevant, compounding reach over time
- Plan 2-4 weeks in advance
- Forms the backbone of the calendar

**Tier 2: Timely/Reactive Content (40%)**
- Seasonal events, holidays, trending topics
- Plan seasonal content 2-4 weeks ahead
- Leave 20% of calendar slots open for real-time trends

### Seasonal Content Calendar Overlay

Layer these onto the pillar rotation:

**Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: New year goals, industry predictions, "state of" reports
**Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Spring campaigns, mid-year planning, conference season
**Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Summer casual content, back-to-school (B2C), Q3 planning
**Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Holiday campaigns, year-in-review, predictions for next year

### How to Integrate Trends Into Pillars

Trends should be filtered through existing pillars, not replace them:

```
Trend: "Brat Summer" audio trending on TikTok

Educational pillar → "3 marketing lessons from Brat Summer"
Entertaining pillar → Brand-relevant Brat Summer meme
Promotional pillar → "Our product is very brat" product showcase
Community pillar → "Is your brand brat or demure? Vote below"
```

### Trend Integration Rules

1. Only participate in trends relevant to your audience
2. Connect the trend back to a content pillar (never trend-jack randomly)
3. Act within 24-48 hours (trends decay fast)
4. Keep 2-3 calendar slots per week unscheduled for reactive content
5. If a trend conflicts with brand values, skip it

## 9. Content Repurposing Strategy

### The Pillar Content Atomization Method

Start with one high-effort "pillar" piece and decompose it into 10-15 platform-native assets:

```
                    ┌─────────────────┐
                    │  PILLAR PIECE   │
                    │  (Blog, Video,  │
                    │   Podcast)      │
                    └────────┬────────┘
                             │
         ┌───────────────────┼───────────────────┐
         │                   │                   │
    ┌────▼─────┐      ┌─────▼────┐       ┌─────▼─────┐
    │ SOCIAL   │      │  VIDEO   │       │  VISUAL   │
    │ POSTS    │      │  CLIPS   │       │  ASSETS   │
    └────┬─────┘      └────┬─────┘       └─────┬─────┘
         │                 │                   │
    ├─ Twitter thread  ├─ TikTok clip     ├─ Infographic
    ├─ LinkedIn post   ├─ Instagram Reel  ├─ Carousel slides
    ├─ Quote cards     ├─ YouTube Short   ├─ Quote graphics
    └─ Poll/question   └─ Story snippets  └─ Checklist image
```

### Repurposing Matrix by Format

| Source Format | Repurpose Into |
|--------------|----------------|
| Blog post | Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, carousel, infographic, email newsletter, video script |
| Long video | 3-5 short clips (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), quote cards, blog post, audiogram |
| Podcast | Short clips, quote graphics, blog transcript, Twitter thread, LinkedIn post |
| Webinar | Blog recap, tutorial clips, slide carousel, email series |
| Case study | Testimonial graphic, video testimonial, Twitter thread, LinkedIn post |
| Data report | Infographic, stat cards, Twitter thread, LinkedIn carousel |

### Repurposing by Pillar

Each pillar piece can yield multiple format variations:

**Educational pillar piece** (e.g., "5 Ways to Improve X"):
- Carousel with all 5 tips (Instagram/LinkedIn)
- Individual tip as separate Reels (Instagram/TikTok)
- Full thread on X
- Long-form LinkedIn article
- Email newsletter deep-dive

**Entertaining pillar piece** (e.g., behind-the-scenes video):
- Full video on YouTube
- 3 short clips for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
- Photo dump carousel on Instagram
- Casual story on LinkedIn
- GIF or meme derivative for X

### Efficiency Gains

- Repurposing improves content ROI by ~32%
- Saves 60-80% of creation time vs. creating from scratch
- Multiplies distribution reach by 3-5x
- Teams that batch-create and repurpose save ~40% of weekly content creation time
